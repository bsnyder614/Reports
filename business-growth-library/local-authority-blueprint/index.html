02 · The Framework

Five Pillars

The Local Authority Framework

Every idea in this playbook rolls up into one of these five roles. Together, they build the reputation that gets you recommended — by people and by AI.

Pillar One
Neighborhood Expert
Become known for understanding the communities you serve — not just the loans, the actual streets, parks, and pockets that make up your market.
Pillar Two
Community Connector
Highlight local businesses and organizations. Every feature you give someone else is a relationship — and a reason for them to talk about you.
Pillar Three
Housing Resource
Teach rather than sell. Provide valuable education consumers can actually use, whether or not they ever become a client.
Pillar Four
Realtor Partner
Create content with other professionals. Become the connector rather than the promoter — the person agents want to feature, not just be featured by.
Pillar Five
Consistent Publisher
Small pieces of valuable content published consistently outperform occasional "perfect" content. Rhythm beats polish.

09 · Your Content Calendar

One Repeatable Rhythm

The 12-Month Local Authority Content Calendar

Instead of 100 disconnected ideas, use one monthly rhythm — four weeks, four roles, repeated for a year. Pull each week's idea from the lists in this playbook.

Week 1
Neighborhood Spotlight
Pull from the 25 Neighborhood Video Ideas. Establish you as the local market expert.
Week 2
Realtor Conversation
Pull from the 25 Realtor Interview Ideas. Deepen a referral relationship on camera.
Week 3
Local Business Feature
Pull from the 25 Business Spotlight Ideas. Give first, build goodwill, earn a share.
Week 4
Market Insight
Pull from Monthly Market Content. Teach rather than sell — a housing-resource moment.

Repeat this four-week cycle for twelve months → 52 pieces of local authority content
